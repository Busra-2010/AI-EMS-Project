LEAVE POLICY:
Employees are entitled to 15 days of casual leave per year.
Casual leave cannot be carried forward to the next year.
Sick leave is granted up to 10 days per year with a medical certificate.
Earned leave accumulates at 1 day per month of service.
Leave requests must be submitted at least 3 days in advance.
Total paid leave per year is 20 days (casual + earned combined).

ATTENDANCE POLICY:
Office hours are 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM, Monday to Friday.
Employees must check in by 9:30 AM. Late arrival is marked as half day.
Work from home is allowed up to 2 days per week with manager approval.

PAYROLL POLICY:
Salaries are processed on the last working day of each month.
PF is deducted at 12% of basic salary each month.
Income tax is deducted at source as per applicable slabs.

APPRAISAL POLICY:
Performance reviews are conducted every 6 months (Q2 and Q4).
Ratings are on a scale of 1 to 5.
A rating of 4.5 or above qualifies for fast-track promotion consideration.
Employees can appeal their rating within 7 days of receiving it.

RESIGNATION AND EXIT POLICY:
Notice period is 30 days for employees below manager level.
Notice period is 60 days for manager level and above.
Full and final settlement is processed within 45 days of last working day.
Experience letter and relieving letter are issued on the last working day.
Employees must return all company assets before exit clearance.
Resignation must be submitted in writing to the HR department.
Notice period can be waived off with mutual agreement between employee and management.

BENEFITS AND PERKS:
Health insurance of Rs 5 lakhs is provided for employee and family.
Provident fund contribution is 12% of basic salary by both employee and employer.
Gratuity is paid after 5 years of continuous service.
Annual leave travel allowance of Rs 25000 is provided.
Mobile reimbursement of Rs 1000 per month for eligible employees.
Internet reimbursement of Rs 500 per month for work from home employees.

CODE OF CONDUCT:
Employees must maintain professional behavior at all times.
Harassment of any kind is strictly prohibited and will result in termination.
Confidential company information must not be shared outside the organization.
Employees must not engage in any conflict of interest activities.
Dress code is business casual from Monday to Thursday and casual on Friday.
Any violation of code of conduct must be reported to HR immediately.

TRAINING AND DEVELOPMENT:
Every employee gets an annual learning budget of Rs 15000 for courses and certifications.
Employees can apply for internal job postings after completing 1 year in current role.
Mandatory training sessions are conducted every quarter.
New employees go through a 30 day onboarding program.

WORK FROM HOME POLICY:
Employees can work from home up to 2 days per week with manager approval.
Full time work from home requires HR and manager approval.
Employees must be available on calls and messages during office hours while working from home.
Company laptop and required equipment will be provided for work from home employees.

GRIEVANCE POLICY:
Employees can raise grievances by emailing hr@company.com.
All grievances will be addressed within 7 working days.
Employees can escalate unresolved grievances to senior HR management.
Anonymous complaints can be submitted through the company grievance portal.
